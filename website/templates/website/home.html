About Us

Bushy Park Tennis Club is a vibrant and exciting tennis club in Terenure.

We believe tennis is for all and shouldn't be locked behind huge membership fees. That's why our club is open to all in the community, just book in online and you can turn up and play tennis on our wonderful courts - no membership required!

Our Club has nine floodlit, all-weather tennis courts, including three artificial clay courts, all kept to the highest standards. Our clubhouse can provide you with everything you need for your tennis match, including racquets, balls, grips and much more. Pop in to the clubhouse and one of our lovely staff members will be happy to help you.

Our club offers a wide range of coaching for all ages and ability levels, so whether you're just starting out, or looking to perfect your game, our excellent coaches have got you covered!
