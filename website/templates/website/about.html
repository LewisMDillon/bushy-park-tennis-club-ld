About Our Club

Beautiful Facilities

Our Club has nine floodlit, all-weather tennis courts, including three artificial clay courts, all kept to the highest standards. Our clubhouse can provide you with everything you need for your tennis match, including racquets, balls, grips and much more. Pop in to the clubhouse and one of our lovely staff members will be happy to help you.

Open To All

Everyone is welcome to play at Bushy Park, and our no-membership model means that you don't have to jump through a thousand hoops in order to play tennis here, just book in online and you can turn up and play tennis on our wonderful courts - no membership required!

Fairly Priced

We pride ourselves on keeping our prices low so that tennis can be enjoyed here by all members of the community. we offer great prices on court rentals, coaching and more so drop in and say hello!
